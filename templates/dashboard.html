Welcome to RELAY!


simple chat app made by Vihan Raman


What is it?

RELAY is a simple chat app where people get to communicate in private rooms. Each private room is randomized, making chats secure.


How does it work?

RELAY runs on a Flask-SocketIO and Flask backend that handles communications between users and makes a safe user experience.


How do I join?

Just click on the button below! You'll be redirected to a home page. Have fun!
